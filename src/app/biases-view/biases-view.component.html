Anchoring Bias
Business Input1
Pricing: Strong dependency on reference price.

Price promotions may backfire beyond the reference price.
Business Input2
Promotions: Not “Cherry Pickers” High Brand Loyalty is expected
Business Input3
Channel: Traditional purchase and decision making using brick & mortar Need to explore other needs like search costs to bring them to digital channel

Business Input4
Ad Content: Focus on benefits due to brand stickiness and loyalty Nostalgic Messages work better
Business Input5
Innovator Traits: Not innovators or early adopters Not a good segment to experiment new products
Business Input6
Social Conformity: Inclined to be with like minded people High dependence on social group.
